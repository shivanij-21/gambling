Frequently Ask Questions

01. What are Betting Exchanges ?

Betting Exchanges are the modern form of online betting platforms where punters can both back and lay bets on sporting and other events. A betting exchange is a platform that allows punters to bet on both sides of an event. This means that you can bet that an event will happen or that it won’t happen. Betting exchanges are different from traditional bookmakers as they don't offer one single odds.

02. Is Sports Betting Software legal ?

Yes, Sports Betting Software is legal and is used by many professional sports bettors. However, it is important to make sure that you are using the software in compliance with any applicable laws in your jurisdiction.
