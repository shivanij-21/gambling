01. What are Betting Exchanges ?

Betting Exchanges are modern form of online betting platforms where punters can both back and lay bets on sporting and other events. A betting exchange is a platform that allows punters to bet on both sides of an event. This means that you can bet that an event will happen or that it won’t happen. Betting exchanges are different from traditional bookmakers as they don't offer one single odds.
